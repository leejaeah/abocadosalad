Our
Story

"아보카도 샐러드" 는 건강과 자연을 담은 샐러드 브랜드로, 푸짐한 채소와 영양소를 품은 식사를 제공합니다. 우리는 지속가능한 농업과 협력하여 고객에게 안전하고 맛있는 경험을 전달하며, 샐러드를 통해 일상에 활력을 불어넣습니다. 함께하는 모든 순간을 건강하고 풍요롭게 채워줄 "아보카도 샐러드" 와 함께하세요.

Brand
Identity

'Abocado Salad' 로고의 'o' 텍스트 속의 컬러원은 우리 브랜드의 열정, 품질, 건강을 상징하며, 고객들에게 제공하는 샐러드의 특징을 시각적으로 강조합니다. 메인 컬러는 신선함을 담은 초록 컬러와 포인트 컬러 주황 컬러를 담아 생동감 넘치고 다채로운 느낌을 담았습니다.회사의 궁극적인 목표와 지향점을 타이포에 담았습니다.

Our Core Values

Health and Well-being

We prioritize our customers' health above all, striving to promote
healthy eating habits through a variety of nutritious menu options.
Our goal is to enhance the quality of life for our customers by
providing nutritious foods that enable them to make healthy choices.
Furthermore, we aim to enrich our customers' lives by fostering a
well-balanced lifestyle through their dining experience.

Freshness

We always use fresh ingredients to deliver the best taste and nutrition.
Additionally, we offer a variety of ingredients and topping options to help
customers create their own custom salads. This approach provides our
customers with a wide range of choices, satisfying both taste and
nutritional needs simultaneously.

Sustainability

We pursue a sustainable business model. Considering both individual
health and the planet's ecosystems and resources, we strive to realize
social value without burdening the environment. By adopting
environmentally friendly ingredient choices and production methods,
we aim for sustainable business operations.

Satisfaction and Experience

We always prioritize our customers' satisfaction. Therefore, we strive
to provide a comfortable and enjoyable dining experience. We value
our customers' feedback and continuously improve our services based
on their input, ensuring that our customers can trust and love our brand.

Community

We value close relationships with our customers and aim to build a
growing community together. Through communication with our customers,
we share our brand's values and strive for the brand's development through
their participation. Our goal is to establish a strong bond between the brand
and our customers, progressing together.
